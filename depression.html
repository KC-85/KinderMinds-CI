Understanding Depression

Depression is a serious mental health condition that affects mood, energy, and daily life. It can impact relationships, work, and overall well-being.

Common Types of Depression:

  • Major Depressive Disorder: A persistent feeling of sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest in activities.
  • Persistent Depressive Disorder (Dysthymia): A long-term but milder form of depression.
  • Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD): Depression triggered by seasonal changes, often during winter months.

Available Treatments:

Depression is treatable through therapy, medications (SSRIs, SNRIs, MAOIs), and lifestyle changes such as exercise and social support.
