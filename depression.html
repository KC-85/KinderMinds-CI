Understanding Depression

Depression is a serious mental health disorder that affects mood, energy, and daily life.

Common Types of Depression

  • Major Depressive Disorder: Persistent sadness, loss of interest, and fatigue.
  • Persistent Depressive Disorder (Dysthymia): Chronic depression lasting two years or more.
  • Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD): Depression triggered by seasonal changes.

Treatments for Depression

Effective treatments include:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Helps challenge negative thinking.
  • Medication: SSRIs and SNRIs can help manage symptoms.
  • Lifestyle Changes: Exercise, healthy diet, and social support.
