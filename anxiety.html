Understanding Anxiety

Anxiety disorders affect millions worldwide, causing excessive worry, rapid heartbeat, and difficulty concentrating.

Common Anxiety Disorders

  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD): Persistent worry about everyday life.
  • Social Anxiety Disorder: Fear of being judged in social situations.
  • Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD): Repetitive thoughts and behaviors.
  • Panic Disorder: Sudden, unexpected panic attacks.

Treatments for Anxiety

There are multiple treatment options available, including:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Helps reframe negative thoughts.
  • Medication: Includes SSRIs like fluoxetine and sertraline.
  • Relaxation Techniques: Meditation, deep breathing, and mindfulness.
