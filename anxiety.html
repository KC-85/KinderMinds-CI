Understanding Anxiety

Recognising the signs and finding ways to manage how you feel.

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a natural response to stress. It can become overwhelming when feelings of worry, fear, or unease persist and start affecting daily life. If anxiety is interfering with sleep, work, or relationships, support is available.

Common Signs & Symptoms

  • Racing thoughts, difficulty concentrating
  • Restlessness or feeling “on edge”
  • Trouble sleeping
  • Rapid heartbeat, shortness of breath
  • Muscle tension, stomach discomfort
  • Avoiding situations that trigger worry

Self-Care Tips

Breathing & Grounding

Slow breathing, counting, and simple grounding (e.g., 5-4-3-2-1) can reduce the intensity of anxious moments.

Small Routines

Regular sleep, meals, and short walks help stabilise mood and energy.

Talk to Someone

Share how you’re feeling with a trusted person. If symptoms persist, consider professional support.
