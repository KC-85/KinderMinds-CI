Domestic Violence and Mental Health

Domestic Violence and Mental Health Issues

Domestic violence can lead to a range of mental health issues including anxiety, PTSD, and depression.

Support for DV-Related Mental Health Issues

Support and Resources

Many individuals experiencing DV-related mental health issues can benefit from:

  • Therapy, such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Support groups and counseling
  • Medication prescribed by specialists

We provide safe spaces for individuals to discuss these topics anonymously and find support.
